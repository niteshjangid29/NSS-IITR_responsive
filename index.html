Our Vision

Our vision to spread social harmony across & evolve student youth as accomplished social leaders & administrators and to bring out solutions across all domains of social service.

Who we are?

NSS IIT Roorkee is the largest student-body of IITR of over 1000 active & dedicated members.We are an organization consisting of 19 well-coordinated cells.

Our Goals

We all are having a common goal of ‘Striving for the betterment of our motherland by fully indulging ourselves in the service of mankind.’Keeping high the ideals of nationalism, secularism & development of scientific temper, we aim to create an impact, abiding by our motto- ‘NOT FOR ME, BUT FOR THE NATION’.

What is it we do?
